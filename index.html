Whether it has been building legos as a young boy, assembling custom computers as a teenager, or developing web-apps currently, I have always enjoyed the challenge of crafting and engineering a product of my own design. The pleasure I glean from every stage of the creative process, drives me to further my knowledge of science and technology. This has been a passion of mine for as long as I can remember, and I believe will continue to be for many years to come.
Music is an important aspect of life, and has been since I first played the drums at the age of nine. The drums led to guitar, and more formal musical training. I believe that the process and discipline required to learn music has translated to many other areas of my life. During my years in college, I had the opportunity to promote live music on campus and manage musical events. Since then, I continue to apply these skills to everyday life, and esspecially to my career in technology.
I have found that the principles I learned while studying Physics, have translated to my abilty to learn programming. To date, I have worked with a large variety of technologies, including: Ruby, JavaScript, PHP, HTML5, CSS3, SQL, as well as many external frameworks and libraries. I enjoy building clean, fast, and creative web-apps for the internet infused world that we live in. Continuing to develop as a software engineer is of great importance to me.
